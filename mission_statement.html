About Us

God Loves Kinderen (GLK) is a nonprofit organization founded by Catherine Martey in March 2016. It obtained a registration status in Ghana in September 2017 with a joint directorate by Benjamin Agyare and Catherine Martey. Ghanaians residing in Ghana, United Kingdom and United States, who are passionate about helping children succeed, contribute financially to the operations of GLK. We love children and our mission is to bring help to street children, children of orphanage homes, children from very poor homes and children with special needs in Ghana. Some children are at a disadvantaged situation through no fault of theirs and may end up at places likely to marginalize their future. We realize the future of our country depends on these children and so it is important we support their upbringing and education and keep them off the streets. We offer financial help to this type of children and at the same time communicate hope to them through Godâ€™s word.

Mission Statement

To provide the poor and vulnerable street children with their material needs of food, water, shelter, education and prepare them for challenges of adulthood and eternity through counseling and teaching them the Word of God.
