Activity Reports

Children of Orphanage Homes

In December 2016, GLK delivered the first support project to the Home of the Motherly Love Foundation in Klagon, near Lashibi in the Greater Accra Region, Ghana. The items GLK donated were 2 banked beds, 2 mattresses and some foodstuffs to the tune of about GhS 1600.00. This exercise was executed by Nicholas Ofei on behalf of GLK.

In December 2017, GLK leadership approved spending of GhS 2700 towards needy children. About GhS 1411 was applied towards the provision of food, toiletries and educational stationery to the Home of the Motherly Love Foundation in Klagon, near Lashibi in the Greater Accra Region. The presentation was done by Judith Fugar, Shirley Martey and Nicholas Ofei. The items were received by Rev. and Mrs. Azumah.

Children from very poor homes

In January 2018, an amount of GhS 600 was used to pay for the registration for two children of a needy family in Adenta, Greater Accra Region so the children could return to school.
