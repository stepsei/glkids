Programs

The objectives of GLK are honed as programs. Currently, we offer the following programs of assistance to needy children in Ghana:

  1. 1. Develop plans and programs to get the underprivileged children back to school with the support of their parents and guardians.
  2. 2. Undertake periodic health screenings for the children in deprived communities.
  3. 3. Share the word of God with the children while feeding them.
  4. 4. Provide the children with clothing and other amenities when necessary.
  6. 5. Provide counseling to children who have need for counseling, especially those who have been sexually abused.
